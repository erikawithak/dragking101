POP! Goes The Easel Creating Promo Graphics for Performers & Producers

Live Date: Sunday, August 3, 2025

Time: 12pm to 3pm (EST)

via Zoom

Reserve Your Spot! - $5

Create unique, eye-catching graphics for your next event!

Step up your promo game! In this virtual workshop, performers and producers will learn how to create eye-catching flyers and social media graphics using Canva, the free and beginner-friendly design platform. Whether you're promoting a show, building your brand, or creating content for IG, this session will cover the basics of effective design and walk you through Canva's essential tools.
