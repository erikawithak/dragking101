Your step-by-step guide to building a show that shines!

A practical and empowering workshop for anyone looking to bring their creative vision to the stage. From drag and burlesque to comedy and live music, this session walks you through the essential steps of producing a successful variety show.

From shaping your initial concept and building a budget to booking performers, marketing the event, and managing the big day. Whether you're a seasoned performer ready to try your hand at producing or someone brand new to the scene, you'll leave with tools, tips, and real-world insights to help turn your ideas into unforgettable experiences.
